About Us

About Us

SortED is a smart Learning Management and Human Resource Management System designed exclusively for schools.

SortED helps schools manage academics, faculty, and student engagement through an integrated platform powered by AI.

  • Streamline academic workflows with our AI-enabled Learning Management System (LMS).
  • Enhance staff productivity using Human Resource Management System (HRMS) tools.
  • Leverage AI-powered reports for predictive GPA modeling, faculty evaluation, and workload analytics.

SortED combines LMS, HRMS, and AI analytics into one powerful solution for schools. From managing digital assignments to tracking faculty attendance and organizing school events, SortED is designed to transform education operations with innovation and simplicity.

Products

Products

Learning Management System (LMS)

AI-powered study plans, instant doubt-solving, gamified quizzes, academic reels, and real-time student progress reports — all tailored for personalized learning.

Human Resource Management System (HRMS)

Manage attendance, leave, payroll, recruitment, and compliance. Gain AI insights into faculty workload, burnout risk, and performance tracking.

AI-Based Reporting

Utilize predictive GPA models, student risk alerts, and subject-wise performance heatmaps. Get explainable insights for smarter academic decisions.

SortED Day & Communities

Monthly events that celebrate talent and collaboration in sports, arts, debate, coding, and more. AI matches students to ideal clubs and tracks progress.
